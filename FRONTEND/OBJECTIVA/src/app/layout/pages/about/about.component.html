ABOUT US

Welcome to OBJECTIVA!

We are a team of student developers dedicated to simplifying Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) in Java. Our platform aims to help learners grasp OOP concepts with ease and confidence.

WHAT WE DO

Our mission is to create an engaging and accessible learning experience for Java's OOP concepts. This platform is part of our capstone project at Holy Angel University, designed to support fellow students in their programming journey.

Thank you for visiting OBJECTIVA. We hope you find our platform helpful and enjoyable.
